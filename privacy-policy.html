Privacy Policy.

Last modified: April 13, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes basic information like your email and name when you create an account, as well as metadata related to the QR codes you generate and your scan analytics. We do not collect sensitive browsing history or contents of your files unless explicitly shared.

2. Data Usage and Processing

Your data is primarily used to facilitate the generation and tracking of QR codes. We utilize scan data (such as approximate location, device type, and timestamp) to provide you with meaningful analytics. We never sell your personal information to advertisers or third-party brokers.

3. Security Protcols

We employ enterprise-grade encryption (TLS 1.3 and AES-256) to secure your data both in transit and at rest. Access to our database is strictly controlled and audited. We regularly perform vulnerability scans to ensure your information remains protected against modern threats.

4. Global Compliance

NQR is built with GDPR and CCPA compliance in mind. You have the right to request a full copy of your data, or its permanent deletion, at any time through our user dashboard or by contacting our legal team.
