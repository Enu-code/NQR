What is the difference between Static and Dynamic QRs?

A Static QR has the destination (URL, text, contact info) hardcoded directly into the pattern of the QR code itself. It cannot be changed once generated. A Dynamic QR routes via our shortlink (e.g., nqr.io/go/xxx), meaning you can log into your dashboard and change where the QR points at any time, even after you've printed it.

Are there scan limits on the free plan?

No. Neither the Free nor the Pro plans have hard limits on the number of times your QR code can be scanned. However, analytics tracking on the free plan is limited to the last 30 days of data.

How secure are File Transfer QRs?

Very secure. Uploaded files are encrypted at rest. When generating a File QR, you can optionally set a password requirement for download, and you can configure the file to automatically delete itself after a certain number of hours or scans.

Can I remove the "Powered by Neverno" branding?

Yes. White-labeling the download landing pages, removing NQR branding, and using custom domains (e.g., qr.yourcompany.com) are features available on our Business plan.

What happens if my subscription expires?

Your static QR codes will continue to function normally forever. Any dynamic QR codes or hosted files linked to your account will be temporarily paused and will show a placeholder page until the subscription is reactivated.
