We protect millions of data points for the world's most security-conscious teams. Your data is your property—we just keep it safe.

Military-Grade Encryption

All data transferred between your device and NQR is protected using TLS 1.3. Files stored on our cluster are encrypted at rest using AES-256 standards, ensuring not even our engineers can access your private data.

Granular Access Control

Control exactly who can view your links and files. Set secure PINs, enforce domain restrictions, or utilize auto-expiry rules that delete your data after a specific time or number of successful scans.

Distributed Infrastructure

NQR runs on a globally distributed, load-balanced infrastructure with SOC-3 and ISO 27001 certifications. We maintain a 99.99% uptime SLA, ensuring your mission-critical QR codes are always online.

GDPR & CCPA Compliant

We take privacy seriously. Our systems are built to fully respect user rights for data deletion, portability, and transparency. No third-party trackers, no selling of data. Just clean, secure technology.
